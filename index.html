How it works

Up and Running in Minutes

Whether you're an NGO or a volunteer, getting started takes less than 5 minutes.

For Volunteers

1
Create your profile

Sign up and tell us your skills, city, and availability. Takes 2 minutes.

2
Discover NGOs

Browse verified NGOs near you. Filter by cause — education, health, disaster relief and more.

3
Get AI-matched

Our AI assigns you to tasks that fit your skills perfectly. Accept, complete, earn points.

4
Earn & rise up

Every task earns you points. Climb the leaderboard and unlock exclusive volunteer badges.

For NGOs

1
Register your NGO

Submit details for verification. Get listed on KarSeva within 24 hours.

2
Post tasks & needs

Describe what help you need — location, skills, urgency. Our AI does the rest.

3
AI assigns volunteers

KarSeva matches the best volunteers to your task automatically with full reasoning shown.

4
Track & manage

Real-time dashboard — who's assigned, what's done, what needs attention right now.
